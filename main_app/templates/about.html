About the Pop! Collector

Pop! Collector is a place where you can log your collection of Funko Pop!s. What's more, there is a built-in cleaning log to keep your collection clean and tidy so that the harmful affects of dust won't damage your items! With this resource you can add Pop!s, edit them if you've made any mistakes, and remove them if they've left your collection. You can also categorize your collection further by adding what group it belongs in. You will have the ability to add groups, edit them, or delete them with no consequence to your collection.

You can be a Pop! Collector too!
